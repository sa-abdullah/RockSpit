Rock Paper Scissors Game!

Game Rules

You must choose one of the three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.

Keep in mind that you are playing against the computer, which randomly selects one of the three options.

The goal is to choose the option that beats the option the computer selects. The game is played in a series of rounds, where each round results in one of the following outcomes:

  • Win
  • Lose
  • Tie

Here are the rules to get a point from the game

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

If the player and computer choose the same option (Ex. Paper and Paper), you Tie. Hence, then no one gets the point.
